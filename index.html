About QIRT ASIA 2027

The Premier Conference on Quantitative Infrared Thermography in Asia

Quantitative Infrared Thermography (QIRT) conference is a biannual international conference, in which Infrared Thermography (IRT) specialists from industry and academia discuss and present their current interests and novel developments. Since 1992, QIRT conferences have been held across the globe, establishing a strong tradition of excellence in thermal imaging research and applications.

Since 2015, the QIRT-Asia series was established to serve the growing Asian community, with previous conferences held in Chennai (India, 2015), Daejeon (Korea, 2017), Tokyo (Japan, 2019), Abu Dhabi (UAE, 2023), and Harbin (China, 2025). The 6th QIRT Asia 2027 will be held in the vibrant city of Singapore, bringing together researchers, engineers, and industry professionals from around the world.
