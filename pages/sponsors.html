Why Sponsor QIRT ASIA 2027?

Connect with the unparalleled community of thermal imaging experts

Targeted Asian Audience

QIRT ASIA focuses on the rapidly growing Asian market for infrared thermography. Sponsors gain direct access to researchers, academics, and industry professionals from across the region, providing a unique platform to establish and strengthen your presence in this key market.

Access to Decision Makers

Meet face-to-face with key decision-makers who utilize thermal imaging technology in sectors ranging from aerospace and manufacturing to medicine and cultural heritage preservation.

Showcase Innovation

Demonstrate your latest cameras, software, and solutions in our exhibition area. The conference atmosphere encourages detailed technical discussions and hands-on demonstrations with an expert audience.

Brand Visibility

Your brand will be featured prominently on all conference materials, including the website, program, and proceedings, associating your company with excellence in scientific research and technological advancement.
