Conference Topics

Scientific Scope and Research Areas

QIRT ASIA 2027 welcomes contributions in all areas of quantitative infrared thermography. The principal topics include, but are not limited to:

  • State-of-the-art & Evolution

    Process and system simulation, experimental validation, calibration, emissivity determination.

  • IR Thermography in Biomedicine

    Medical diagnostics, physiological monitoring, veterinary medicine.

  • NDT & Material Analysis

    Defect detection, material characterization, composite structures inspection.

  • Fluid Dynamics & Energetics

    Flow visualization, heat transfer analysis, combustion processes.

  • Civil Engineering & Buildings

    Building diagnostics, energy efficiency, cultural heritage preservation.

  • Industrial Applications

    Process monitoring, quality control, preventative maintenance.

  • Remote Sensing & Environment

    Environmental monitoring, aerial thermography, urban heat islands.

  • Induction & Laser Thermography

    Advanced active thermography techniques and applications.
