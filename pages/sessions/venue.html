Holiday Inn Singapore Atrium

Located in the heart of the city, just steps away from the Singapore River, Holiday Inn Singapore Atrium is a spectacular 27-story atrium hotel that offers a unique blend of modern comfort and traditional warmth.

  • 317 Outram Road, Singapore 169075
  • +65 6733 0188
  • Near Havelock MRT Station

Access

The hotel is approximately a 25-minute taxi ride from Changi International Airport. It is also well-connected by public transport, with the Havelock MRT station just a short walk away.

Venue Highlights

Iconic Architecture

Distinctive cylindrical design offering panoramic city views.

Grand Atrium Lobby

Spectacular 27-story atrium with glass capsule elevators.
