Dining in Singapore

A Gastronomic Paradise

Must-Try Local Dishes

  • Chilli Crab - Singapore's national seafood dish.
  • Hainanese Chicken Rice - Fragrant rice with poached chicken.
  • Laksa - Spicy coconut noodle soup.
  • Satay - Grilled skewered meat with peanut sauce.

Recommended Areas

Clarke Quay & Boat Quay

Riverside dining with a wide variety of seafood restaurants and bars. Perfect for evening networking.

Maxwell Food Centre

Famous hawker centre offering authentic and affordable local street food. Try the Tian Tian Chicken Rice.
