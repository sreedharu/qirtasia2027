01 / The venue

A conference centre built for scientific gatherings at the heart of Southeast Asia's research hub.

Sands Expo & Convention Centre occupies the second of Marina Bay Sands' three towers, purpose-built to host international conferences. QIRT Asia 2027 will take place on Level 4, with a dedicated plenary hall, six parallel session rooms, an industry exhibition floor, and a connected foyer for posters and refreshments.

The venue is directly connected to Marina Bay Sands hotel accommodation, The Shoppes, restaurants, and the Bayfront MRT interchange — delegates can move between sessions, meals and lodging without leaving the complex.

03 / Around the venue

Things to
explore.

A few minutes from the conference hall, delegates will find gardens, museums, and some of the city's most celebrated cuisine.

Gardens by the Bay
A 101-hectare horticultural landmark across the bay, featuring the Supertree Grove and two biodomes.
ArtScience Museum
Singapore's museum of art, science and technology — a short walk from the conference venue.
Lau Pa Sat
Historic hawker centre in the CBD — a UNESCO-recognised food culture, minutes away by MRT.
Merlion Park
The iconic waterfront viewpoint across the bay, featuring Singapore's symbolic half-lion, half-fish statue.
National Gallery
Southeast Asia's largest modern art collection, housed in the restored former Supreme Court building.
Chinatown & Telok Ayer
Historic quarter of shophouses, temples and some of the city's finest dining, one MRT stop away.
